Instructions for Hypermetropia Test


  1. Ensure you are in well-lit room
  2. Take off your glasses or contact lenses
  3. Ensure your eyes are clean
  4. For myopia (farsightedness), keep camera at approx 35 cms.
  5. Cover one eye but not to press on it
